# Reopen a safety incident or observation

If a safety incident or observation requires further investigation after it was closed or canceled, you can reopen it to work on it again.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ohs\_im.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Health and Safety** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace**.

2.  Select the incident management icon \(![Incident Management icon.](../image/list-icon-hs.png)\).

3.  In the **Lists** tab, open the safety incident or observation list.

    -   For the safety incidents list, select **Safety Incidents** and then **All**.
    -   For the safety observations list, select **Safety Observations** and then **All**.
4.  Open the safety incident or observation to reopen and change the state to **Work in Progress**.


## Result

The safety incident or observation is reopened.

## What to do next

Work on the safety incident or observation to complete it.
