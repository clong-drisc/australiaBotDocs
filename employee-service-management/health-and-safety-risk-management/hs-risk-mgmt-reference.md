# Health and Safety Risk Management reference

Using the reference topics, learn about the components, such as the roles, tables, plugins, and store applications that are installed when you install the Health and Safety Risk Management application. Reference topics also provide additional information about the lists and forms that you use to configure and use Health and Safety Risk Management.

-   **[Components installed with Health and Safety Risk Management](../reference/components-installed-with-hs-risk-mgmt.md)**  
Several types of components are installed with installation of the Health and Safety Risk Management application, including tables, user roles, and scheduled jobs. The application also installs related store applications if they aren’t already installed.
-   **[Submit JSA form](../reference/hs-submit-jsa-form.md)**  
Employees can use the Submit JSA form to analyze their job safety at their point of work and create a JSA.
-   **[Risk matrix form](../reference/hs-risk-matrix-form.md)**  
Safety team can use the risk matrixes to create and conduct risk assessments in the Health and Safety Workspace.
-   **[Risk assessment form](../reference/hs-risk-assessment-form.md)**  
Risk assessment managers can use the risk assessment form to conduct their risk assessments.
-   **[Hazard assessment form](../reference/hs-hazard-assessment-form.md)**  
Risk assessment managers can use the hazard assessment form to review a hazard and choose their control measures and assess their risk levels.
-   **[Inspection schedule form](../reference/hs-inspection-schedule-form.md)**  
Safety inspection manager can use the inspection schedule form to set up a recurring workplace safety inspection.
-   **[Audit schedule form](../reference/hs-audit-schedule-form.md)**  
The safety audit manager can use the audit schedule form to set up a recurring workplace safety audit.
-   **[Audit questionnaire form](../reference/hs-audit-survey-form.md)**  
The safety audit manager can use the audit questionnaire form to assign a smart assessment or survey to be conducted as part of a safety audit.
-   **[Objective form for safety audits](../reference/hs-audit-objectives-form.md)**  
The safety audit manager can use the audit objectives form to define the objectives and scope of a safety audit.
-   **[Finding form for safety audits](../reference/hs-audit-finding-form.md)**  
When an audit's item for review is complete, the safety auditor can use the Findings form to log findings gathered from the review or survey responses.
-   **[Action schedule form for an exposure](../reference/hs-action-schedule-form.md)**  
The industrial hygienist can use the action schedule form for an action to be created when there's an active exposure.
-   **[Permit-to-work request form](../reference/hs-permit-to-work-form.md)**  
Description of the form to request a permit to work.
-   **[Choice new record form](../reference/hs-choice-new-record-form.md)**  
Field description to add a new choice to create a new permit type.
-   **[Variable set new record form](../reference/hs-variable-set-new-record-form.md)**  
Field description to create a new variable set record.
-   **[Variable new record form](../reference/hs-variable-new-record.md)**  
Field description to create a new variable record.
-   **[Domain separation and Health and Safety Risk Management](hs-risk-mgmt-domain-separation.md)**  
Domain separation is supported for Health and Safety Risk Management. Domain separation enables you to separate data, processes, and administrative tasks into logical groupings called domains. You can control several aspects of this separation, including which users can see and access data.
