# Manage the recipients of a communication task

Add or edit the list of recipients for various communication tasks.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_critical.agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace** and select the **Critical Event Management** tab.

2.  Open the critical event.

3.  Select the **Notification center** tab and then select the **Manage recipients** button.

4.  In the **Communication plan** drop-down menu, select the communication task for which you want to manage recipients.

5.  In the form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Add recipient|User, group, or recipient list who receives the communication task.|
    |Designate role|Role that is assigned the user, group, or recipient listed in the **Add recipient** field.|
    |Audience|List of potentially impacted people or important people who receives the communication task.|

6.  Select **Save**.

    **Note:** You can also open the Manage recipients form by selecting the More actions icon \(![More actions icon](../../legal-request-management/image/more-actions-icon.png)\) on the communication task.
