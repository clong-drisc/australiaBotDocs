# Add members to the group

Add members to the group mapped to a location.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Security** &gt; **User and groups** &gt; **Groups**.

2.  Open the relevant group to add members.

3.  Select the **Group members** tab.

4.  Select **New**.

5.  On the form, fill in the fields and select **Submit**.

    **Note:** For more information to fill a new member form, see [New member form](../reference/new-member-form.md).
