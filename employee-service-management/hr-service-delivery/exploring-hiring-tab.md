# Exploring Hiring

Learn more about the Hiring application and review the benefits it can provide to the hiring managers in your organization.

## Hiring overview

The Hiring application provides a unified and seamless experience for managing hiring needs, allowing hiring managers to track job requisitions and applications efficiently. It facilitates collaboration with recruiters and offers clear insights into responsibilities, streamlining the hiring process.

## Hiring users

|User|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Hiring manager|Hiring managers request job openings, track jobs, collaborate with recruiters, view and compare applications, provide feedback, and so on.|
