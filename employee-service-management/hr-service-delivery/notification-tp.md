# Email notification for Talent pools

Notification emails are sent to the recruiters who have Talent pools shared or collaborated with by the owner.

Receive a notification when you’re added as a collaborator in a Talent pool by the pool owner.

Receive a notification when a Talent pool is shared with you to view the details by the owner.
