# View track approval for Workday Learning

Track every pull that has been performed from ServiceNow to Workday Learning. View or edit details of the job, such as when did the job begin and end, when did the records start loading into staging tables, or did the job run completely or fail in between.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lrn\_workday.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workday Learning** &gt; **Data Sources** &gt; **Track Approval**.

2.  Select **Track Approval for Workday Learning**.

3.  On the form, edit the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see [Edit a record in HR Service Delivery Integration with Workday Learning](../reference/sync-user-activity-form.md).
