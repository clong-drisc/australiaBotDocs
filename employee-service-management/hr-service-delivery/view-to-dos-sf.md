# View to dos

View details of the tasks that are pulled from HR Service Delivery Integration with SuccessFactors into ServiceNow application.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sf.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Integrations Framework** &gt; **Todos**

2.  On the form, view the fields.
