# View tasks from Workday

View details of the todos or tasks that are pulled from Workday into ServiceNow.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin, sn\_hr\_workday.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workday Pull To-Dos** &gt; **Workday To-Dos**

2.  On the form, view the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Integration todo ID|Unique identifier for the Workday task.|
    |Active|Option indicating whether the task is active in Workday.|
    |Assigned to|User to whom the task is assigned.|
    |Todo state|Current state of the task in ServiceNow. For example, **Open**, and **Complete**.|
    |URL|URL that navigates to Workday to complete the task.|
    |Todo description|Description of the Workday task visible in ServiceNow Employee Service Center .|
    |Due date|Date and time by which the task has to be completed.|
    |External status|Current status of the task in Workday. For example, **Awaiting Action**, and **Cancelled**.|
    |Extension system|Option indicating the origin of the task, that is Workday.|
