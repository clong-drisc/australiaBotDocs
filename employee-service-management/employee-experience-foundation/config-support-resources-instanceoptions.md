# Modify the Support resources widget display

The widget displays the support resources for the Employee Center Pro Kiosk user. You can configure the instance options based on your display preferences.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sp.esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Employee Center Pro Kiosk home page.

    The kiosk home page is displayed.

2.  Click the **Support resources** widget.

3.  Press and hold the control key on the keyboard, right-click the widget, and then select **Instance Options**.

4.  Configure the instance options.
