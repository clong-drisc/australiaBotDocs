# Modify Send to self widget display

The Send to self widget displays the options for sending useful information as an email or an SMS for the Employee Center Pro Kiosk user. You can configure the instance options based on your display preferences.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sp.esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to Employee Center Pro Kiosk home page.

    The kiosk home page is displayed.

2.  Click the **Send to self** widget.

3.  Press and hold the control key on the keyboard, right-click the Upcoming shift widget, and then select **Instance Options**.

4.  Configure the instance options.

    |Instance option|Description|
    |---------------|-----------|
    |Enable Send SMS|Select the SMS option to send information.|
    |Enable Send Email|Select the Email option to send information.|

5.  Click **Save**.
