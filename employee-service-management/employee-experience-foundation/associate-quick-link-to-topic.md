# Associate a quick link to a topic

Associate quick links to topics to quickly view important content, such as knowledge articles, catalog, items, internal pages, and external links.

## Before you begin

Role required: taxonomy\_admin

User criteria required: Taxonomy Manager.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Content Taxonomy** &gt; **Topics**.

2.  Click and open the topic to associate with a quick link.

3.  On the **Quick Link** tab, click **New**.

    ![Associating topic to the related tabs](../images/associate-topics-tabs.png "Quick link tab")

4.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    For more information on the fields and description, see [Quick Link form](../reference/ec-quick-link-form.md).

    **Note:** You can't add a quick link as a featured item.

5.  Click **Submit**.
