# Modify the Time off widget display

The widget displays the time off balances. You can configure the instance options based on your display preferences.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sp.esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to Employee Center Pro kiosk home page.

    The kiosk home page is displayed.

2.  Click the **Time off** widget.

3.  Press and hold the control key on the keyboard, right-click the time off widget, and then select **Instance Options**.

4.  Configure the **Title** instance options.

    Specify the title of the widget. Default: `Time off`.

    **Note:** Leave balance is rounded off.

5.  Click **Save**.

    **Note:** The time off information is available only after relogging.
