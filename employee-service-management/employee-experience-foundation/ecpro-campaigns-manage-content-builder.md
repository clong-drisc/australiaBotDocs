# Add content to a campaign using Content Experience Builder

Create or place content into a bundle or stage for a campaign using Content Experience Builder. The Content library holds existing content and is organized by content type.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ca.campaign\_manager

Complete the steps to [Create a campaign](ecpro-create-campaign.md) and [Create campaign stages with Content Experience Builder](ecpro-campaign-builder.md)

## About this task

You can add content to the campaign from the Content Library or create new content using the Content Experience Builder.

**Note:** You must create rich content in the Content Library Rich Content Editor, since the Content Experience Builder does not currently support rich content creation. See [Microsites](../concept/ec-rich-content.md)

## Procedure

1.  To open a campaign in the Content Experience Builder do steps 1 and 2. If you have already opened the campaign in the Content Experience Builder, proceed to step 3.
2.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Content Experiences** &gt; **Content Experience Builder**.

3.  Select the campaign and click the **Schedule of content** tab or click **Next**.

    ![Schedule of content tab](../images/campaign-content-builder-1.png)

4.  To create new content or select content from the Content library, click the accordion icon \(![accordion icon](../../human-resources/image/playbook-accordion-icon.png)\) next to a content type.

    A list of existing content appears. You can drag-and-drop the content into a stage. ![Campaign content - Drag content into a stage](../images/campaign-content-drag-drop.png)

    You can use the Filter field to search for content by title \(for example "Travel policy update"\) or content type \(such as Mobile or Portal content\).

    Click **Create new** to create new content. For more information on the New Content form fields, refer to the links in the table below:

## What to do next

-   Continue adding stages and content to the campaign
-   Use Campaign preview to see how the campaign will appear to the audience. See [Campaign preview](../concept/ecpro-campaign-preview.md)
-   If you are creating more than one campaign with similar features, use the **Clone campaign** button to clone the campaign, stages, and content.
-   When you are ready to publish the campaign, click **Publish**.
