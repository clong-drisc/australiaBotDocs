# Employee Slate for Now Assist

Now Assist integration in Employee Slate provides AI-powered assistance for everyday work tasks, including intelligent suggestions, automated workflows, and contextual support.

Now Assist integration provides intelligent assistance throughout your Employee Slate experience. The AI assistant offers proactive suggestions, automates routine tasks, and provides contextual help based on your chat conversations and activities.

## Key features

Now Assist in Employee Slate provides:

-   Intelligent content recommendations
-   Automated form completion and data entry assistance
-   Workflow optimization suggestions
-   Predictive text and smart replies across KBs, catalogs, tasks, requests, and others
-   Task prioritization and scheduling assistance
-   Integration with calendar and productivity tools
