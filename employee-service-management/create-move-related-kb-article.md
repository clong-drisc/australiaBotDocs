# Create a move-related knowledge base article

Create a knowledge base article so that you can provide move-related information or notices to employees.

## Before you begin

Ensure that you have the following applications installed:

-   Workplace Core
-   Workplace Case Management.

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.admin or sn\_wsd\_core.kb\_writer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Core** &gt; **Knowledge management** &gt; **Create new article**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.

</td></tr><tr><td>

## Result

The knowledge base article is created.
