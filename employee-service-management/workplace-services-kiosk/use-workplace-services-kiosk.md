# Using Workplace Services Kiosk

Workplace Services Kiosk provides a seamless check-in, check-out, and self-registration experience for visitors.

-   **[Check in using an invite](../task/visitor-check-in.md)**  
Check in as a visitor by using an invite from an employee. If visitors have an existing invite, they can check in to the kiosk using their passcode, email, phone number, or name.
-   **[Self-register as a visitor](../task/visitor-self-register.md)**  
Register as a visitor by providing your details such as your name, purpose of the visit, and host.
-   **[Using Kiosk Indoor Mapping](using-kiosk-indoor-map.md)**  
Use the kiosk indoor mapping feature to search for spaces, get directions, and raise cases.
-   **[Make workplace reservations on the kiosk](../task/rsv-space-kiosk-map.md)**  
Workplace visitors and guests with kiosk viewer role can make workplace reservations. They can search and select a space on the Kiosk Map to reserve a space.
