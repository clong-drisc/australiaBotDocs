# Reopen a job requisition

Reopen a job requisition that is already in closed state.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  Open a requisition that is in closed state and must be reopened.

3.  Select **Reopen**.

    -   The requisition moves to the **New** state after it's reopened.
    -   For more information on the state management system, see [Job requisition state management](../reference/job-req-state-mngmnt.md).
