# Submit a job requisition for approval

Submit a job requisition for approval to higher management.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment Workspace**.

2.  Select **New** from the **Active job requisition requests by state** card.

3.  Select a job requisition displayed on the Job Requisitions table filtered for state **New**.

    The job requisition opens in a new tab.

4.  On the Job requisition approval request form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see submit for approval form.

5.  Select **Submit for approval**.


## Result

The approval request shows up on the approvers **My tasks** open requests.
