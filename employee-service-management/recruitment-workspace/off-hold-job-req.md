# Withdraw hold on a job requisition

Withdraw the hold put on a job requisition when it is needed.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  Select a job requisition that you want to resume working on.

3.  Select **Resume**.


## Result

The job requisition is off hold and ready to be taken forward.
