# Open a job requisition in your ATS

Open a job requisition in your ATS after it’s approved and ready.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  From the list of job requisitions on the dashboard, open a requisition that is in **Ready** state.

3.  From the ellipses on the top-right corner, select **Open requisition**.


## Result

The requisition is in **Open** state. It’s open to the ATS and can be taken forward for further hiring process.

For more information on the integration with your ATS, see [Integrate with your ATS](../concept/integrate-ats.md).
