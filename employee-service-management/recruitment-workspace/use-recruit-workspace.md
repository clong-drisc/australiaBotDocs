# Using Recruitment workspace

Use the Recruitment workspace to streamline your hiring tasks as a recruiter.

-   **[Get overview of recruitment workload](recruitment-workspace-oveview.md)**  
Recruiters can use the Recruitment workspace overview section to have enhanced visibility into their workload that prospects to effective prioritization of work.
-   **[Create a job requisition as a recruiter](../task/request-job-hiring.md)**  
Request for a job requisition for a vacancy you want to fill in your organization.
-   **[Add a job description to a requisition](../task/add-job-description.md)**  
Add a job description to a requisition, using job description templates.
-   **[Approve a job requisition in the Employee Center](../task/approve-a-job-req.md)**  
Approve a job requisition from My tasks in the Employee Center.
-   **[Track a job requisition in the Recruitment workspace](job-req-details-re-workspace.md)**  
Track applications on a job requisition, view job details, manage the hiring team, and add or remove interview phases.
-   **[Send an email from a job requisition](../task/send-email-to-applicants.md)**  
As a recruiter, send an email to your applicants on a job requisition and provide personalized details.
-   **[Manage hiring team](manage-hiring-team.md#)**  
Add, edit, or remove hiring team members from a job requisition.
-   **[Manage the job requisitions as a recruiter](../../employee-center/concept/manage-job-reqs.md)**  
Update, put it on hold, send a requisition for approval, or close it.
-   **[Tracking an application in the Recruitment workspace](application-record-details-re-workspace.md)**  
Track an application for a job requisition and its corresponding applicant and interview details as a recruiter or recruitment coordinator.
-   **[Hiring tasks](applicant-tasks-recruitr.md)**  
Assign tasks to your job applicants or internal stakeholders and manage those within the Recruitment workspace.
-   **[Collaborate with the hiring manager](../task/collab-hiring-manager.md)**  
Collaborate with the hiring manager for the job requisition on the activity stream.
-   **[Task email notifications for recruiters](email-notice-recruiter-tasks.md)**  
The email notifications provide alerts to the recruiters when a task is completed by an applicant.
-   **[View the Talent Acquisition Dashboard](../task/access-kpi-ta.md)**  
Understand the hiring trend in your organization with the Talent Acquisition Dashboard.
