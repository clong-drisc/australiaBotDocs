# View the Talent Acquisition Dashboard

Understand the hiring trend in your organization with the Talent Acquisition Dashboard.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter and itil

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Platform Analytics** &gt; **Dashboards** &gt; **Talent Acquisition Dashboard**.

2.  View the metrics available on the dashboard to understand your hiring trend.

    For more information, see [Talent Acquisition Dashboard](../concept/kpi-dshbrd-ta.md).
