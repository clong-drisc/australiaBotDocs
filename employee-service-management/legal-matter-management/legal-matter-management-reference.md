# Legal Matter Management reference

Reference topics provide additional information about the lists and forms that you use in Legal Matter Management.

-   **[Components installed with Legal Matter Management](installed-with-legal-matter-management.md)**  
Several types of components are installed with installation of the Legal Matter Management application, including tables, and user roles.
-   **[Practice area tables for legal matters](legal-matter-pa-tables.md)**  
Tables specific to practice areas are installed with the Legal Matter Management application.
-   **[Legal Tracker Outside Counsel Engagement request form](../../legal-tracker/reference/create-lt-outside-counsel-engagement-form.md)**  
Fields in the Legal Tracker Outside Counsel Engagement request form of a legal matter.
-   **[Scheduled jobs installed for Legal Tracker](../../legal-tracker/reference/lmm-lt-sch-jobs.md)**  
Scheduled jobs that are installed when the Legal Tracker spoke is enabled. These jobs are used to fetch data from Legal Tracker.
-   **[Outside counsel engagement notifications](../../legal-tracker/reference/lmm-lt-notifications.md)**  
Notifications are reminders that are created using email templates and sent for outside counsel engagement workflow. Use the notifications received to work on the outside counsel request, tasks and resolve errors.
