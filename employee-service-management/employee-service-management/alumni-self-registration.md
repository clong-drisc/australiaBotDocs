# Register on the Alumni Center website

As an external user, sign up using a personal email address and other details to self-register as alumni.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_asc.alumni

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to Alumni Center website.

2.  Select **Register as alumni** on the login page.

3.  Fill in the fields on the alumni self-registration form.

## Result

A mail is sent to your personal email, which contains a verification link. After you select the link, a staging record is created for you so that the admin can verify your details and move the record to Approved or Rejected status.

-   If approved, the user ID and password are communicated to you via the personal email address provided.
-   If rejected, you receive an email to fill in the required details by selecting to register again, and the verification process is repeated.
