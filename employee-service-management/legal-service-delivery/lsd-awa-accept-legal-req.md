# Accept incoming legal request

As a legal fulfiller, accept or reject to an incoming legal request assignment from the Legal Counsel Center Inbox.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Select the Inbox icon \(![Inbox icon](../image/lsd-awa-inbox.png)\).

3.  Select **Available** from **Status** to indicate that you are available.

4.  When a legal request assignment comes through the chat queue, accept or reject the **Accept**.
