# View contract requests

When a legal request is submitted a contract request associated with it is initiated.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_contracts.contracts\_fulfiller

## About this task

You can access contract request through the legal request or directly from the Legal Counsel Center.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Select the list icon \(![List icon](../../legal-request-management/image/lsd-lcc-list-icon.png)\).

3.  In the Lists tab, navigate to **Contract Requests**.

    -   Select **Assigned to me** to see the contract requests assigned to you.
    -   Select **Collaborations** to see the contract requests where you are a collaborator.
    -   Select **All** to see all the contract requests.
