# Using the Talent Development Core application

Managers and employees can create and track career development and growth plans. Managers access Talent from Employee Center menus **Your career** &gt; **Growth plans**to track employee development.

-   **[Create a growth plan with the help of Now Assist](../task/egd-create-growth-plan-with-nowassist.md)**  
Use Now Assist to help create a growth plan to give your career goals guidance and structure.
-   **[Create a growth plan](../task/egd-create-growth-plan.md)**  
Create a growth plan to give your career goals guidance and structure.
-   **[Edit a growth plan](../task/egd-edit-growth-plan.md)**  
Edit, set a due date, or add additional activities to a growth plan.
-   **[Create a growth plan from an aspiration or main skill](../task/egd-create-growth-plan-master-skill.md)**  
Create a growth plan based on one of your aspirations or main skill.
-   **[Create a growth plan from an aspiration](../task/egd-create-growth-plan-new-role.md)**  
Create a growth plan based on one of your aspirations to help you move towards a new role.
-   **[Create a growth plan from discussions](../task/egd-create-growth-plan-aspiration-type.md)**  
Create a growth plan based on your discussions or aspirations.
-   **[View and edit employees' growth plans](../task/egd-view-edit-employees-growth-plans.md)**  
Review your employees' growth plans to keep track of their progress and help them in their career development.
-   **[Create a Talent Development Aspiration](../task/egd-create-aspirations.md)**  
Create Aspirations to define and track professional and personal interests.
-   **[View Credly badges](../../human-resources/task/view-credly-badges.md)**  
As an employee, sync external badges into your organization’s Credly account and view them on your career profile.
