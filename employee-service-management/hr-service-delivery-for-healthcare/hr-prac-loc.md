# Practice location form

Access, update, and validate the practice location details of a healthcare professional in the Practice location form.

Description

</td><td>

Option to add a professional reference.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Office schedule

Working schedules of the health care professional, for example, 24 X 7.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Accept new patients

</td><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

Active

</td><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

Is current location

</td><td>

Option to indicate that this is the current practice location of the health care professional.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Is billing electronic

</td><td>

Option to indicate that electronic billing is generated at the affiliated hospital for the services rendered by healthcare professional.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Send correspondence here

</td><td>

Option to indicate that any correspondence \(examples: letters\) to the healthcare professional can be sent to the affiliated hospital.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Status

</td><td>

Status of the practice location record: -   New: When profile intake form is sent to the healthcare professional for filling.
-   Pending validation: When data is filled in by healthcare professional and sent for credentialing and validation to HR agents.
-   Invalid: When HR agents review the data and reject due to insufficient or invalid details.
-   Valid: When HR agents validate and approve the information provided by the healthcare professional.
