# View risk assessment details

Review the risk assessment results for a conflict of interest disclosure, including the overall risk score, risk level, individual risk factor responses, and the risk assessment report.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.coi\_fulfiller or sn\_lg\_ops.request\_fulfiller

## About this task

When an employee submits a COI disclosure, the risk assessment evaluates the request against configured risk factors and assigns an overall risk score and risk level. As a fulfiller, you can view the assessment results to understand why a request was scored at a particular level, review individual risk factor responses, and access the risk assessment report.

## Procedure

1.  View risk level and risk responses from the legal request.

    1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

    2.  Open the COI disclosure request.

    3.  View the risk level assigned by the risk assessment from the secondary header.

        ![View the risk level for the submitted request](../image/lsd-coi-risk-level.png)

2.  View risk responses.

    1.  Select the **Risk responses** tab to view the individual risk factor evaluation results.

        The Risk responses tab displays a list of all risk factor evaluations performed for this request. The count in the tab label indicates the number of risk factors evaluated.

        ![View risk responses](../image/lsd-coi-risk-response-lcc.png "Risk responses")

    2.  Select the risk response to see details.

3.  Select the **Risk assessment report** tab to detailed report.

    ![View risk assessment report](../image/lsd-coi-view-report.png)

4.  View overall risk assessment score.

    1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal and Contracts Common Utilities** &gt; **Overall Risk Assessment Scores**.

    2.  Select the record associated with the legal request number for which you want to view the overall risk assessment.
