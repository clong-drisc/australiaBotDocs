# Create workplace contracts in Workplace Central

As a lease administrator or manager, you can create workplace contracts and also add assets, terms and conditions, contract locations, and documents to a contract.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_la.workplace\_central\_user, sn\_wsd\_la.manager or sn\_wsd\_la.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Workplace Central**.

2.  Select **Lease Administration**.

3.  Click **New**.

4.  In the **Contract Model** field, select **Lease**.

5.  In the **Terms and conditions** section, specify the legal information in the contract.

    For more information, see [Add and build terms and conditions in Workplace Central](wpc-lease-add-tc.md)

6.  On the form, fill in the rest of the fields.

    For description of the field values, see [Workplace contract new record form](../reference/workplace-contract-form.md).

7.  Click **Save**.

8.  You can submit the contract for review or add contract services, contract locations, assets, terms and condition, or documents to the contract.

    For more information, see the following topics:

    -   [Add a workplace contract service in Workplace Central](wpc-add-lease-contract-service.md)
    -   [Add workplace contract locations in Workplace Central](wpc-add-workplace-location.md)
    -   [Add assets to a workplace contract in Core UI](add-an-asset-to-a-lease-contract.md)
    -   [Add documents to a workplace contract in Workplace Central](wpc-add-doc-to-lease-cont.md)
9.  Either save the contract or submit it for review.

    -   Click **Save** to save the contract.
    -   Click **Submit for Review** to send notification to the approver.

        For information about the approval process, see [Approve or reject workplace contract in Workplace Central](wpc-approve-lease-contract.md).


-   **[Add a workplace contract service in Workplace Central](wpc-add-lease-contract-service.md)**  
Workplace contract services are the common services offered as part of the lease contract. You can add and track the services along with their associated costs.
-   **[Add workplace contract locations in Workplace Central](wpc-add-workplace-location.md)**  
Add locations to the workplace contract for the locations the contract covers.
-   **[Add asset to workplace contract in Workplace Central](wpc-add-asset-lease-cont.md)**  
Add assets to the workplace contracts.
-   **[Add workplace contract clause in Workplace Central](wpc-add-workplace-contract-clauses.md)**  
Add workplace contract clauses, including insurance clause, parking clause, warranty clauses to store the lease contract clauses.
-   **[Add workplace contract options in Workplace Central](wpc-add-workplace-contract-options.md)**  
Add workplace contract options such as option to renew, option to sub lease, option to terminate to store the lease contract options.
-   **[Add workspace contract responsibilities in Workplace Central](wpc-add-workspace-contract-responsibilities.md)**  
Add workplace responsibilities to indicate who \(landlord or tenant\) is responsible for different activities such as lawn maintenance, painting, repairing.
-   **[Add and build terms and conditions in Workplace Central](wpc-lease-add-tc.md)**  
After you create a terms and conditions record, add the record to a lease contract that is in the Awaiting Review, Rejected, or Renewal Rejected substate.
-   **[Add documents to a workplace contract in Workplace Central](wpc-add-doc-to-lease-cont.md)**  
Add documents to the workplace contract to maintain all the relevant documents in a single place and maintain different versions of the document.
