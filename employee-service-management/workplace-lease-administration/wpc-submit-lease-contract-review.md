# Submit a workplace contract for review in Workplace Central

You can send a contract that is in Draft state for approval.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_la.workplace\_central\_user, sn\_wsd\_la.manager or sn\_wsd\_la.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Workplace Central**.

2.  Select **Lease Administration**.

3.  Select a contract in **Draft** state.

4.  Select an **Approver** for the contract.

5.  Click **Submit For Review**.


## Result

An email message is sent to the selected approver and the contract substate changes to Under Review.
