# Set in-office days

As an employee, using the Workplace Service Portal and the Employee Center, you can set your presence at the office.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.workplace\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the employee presence dashboard from one of the following locations.

**Workplace Service Portal**

</td><td>

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Core** &gt; **Workplace service portal**.

The portal homepage opens.

2.  Open the Site safety category.

    1.  Select **Catalog**.
    2.  Select **Browse by Categories**.
    3.  In the Workplace Services Catalog, select **Site safety**.
**Note:** If there are multiple catalogs configured on the portal, go to the Catalogs list and select **Workplace Services Catalog** &gt; **Site safety**.

3.  Select **Employee Presence**.


</td></tr><tr><td id="d762957e136">

**Employee Center**

</td><td>

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.
2.  On the Employee Center landing homepage, select the **Workplace Services** &gt; **Site Safety**.
3.  Select **Employee Presence**


</td></tr></tbody>
</table>2.  On the Presence dashboard page, select **Manage your settings** to open the Add Routine window.

    **Note:** If you’re visiting the dashboard for the first time, select **Get Started**.

3.  Under the In-office days section, select the days of the week when you’re planning to be at the office.

    For example, if you're planning to be in the office on Monday and Wednesday, select **MO** and **WE**.

4.  After setting the days, select **Save**.

    The selection is saved.

5.  You can also add a co-worker as your collaborator and view on what days they’re present in the office.

    For more information, refer to [Add a collaborator](add-a-collaborator.md).


## Result

You have set your in-office days. At any time, you can modify your selection, for more information, refer to [Update your in-office days](update-your-in-office-days.md).
