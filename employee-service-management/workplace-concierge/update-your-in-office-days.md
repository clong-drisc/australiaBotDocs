# Update your in-office days

Update your in-office days at any time using the Presence dashboard.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.workplace\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Presence dashboard.

    For more information, see the steps mentioned [here](set-in-office-days.md#choicetable_presence_dashboard).

2.  On the Presence dashboard, open the Add Routine window by selecting **Manage your settings**.

3.  In the In-office days section, change your existing selections based on your requirement.

    -   Select a day to set your in-office presence.
    -   Clear a selection to set a remote presence.
4.  After setting the days, select **Save**.

    The selection is updated.
