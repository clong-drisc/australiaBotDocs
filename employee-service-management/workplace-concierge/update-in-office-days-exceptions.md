# Update your in-office days as exceptions

As an employee, use the presence dashboard to update your in-office days without changing your routine.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.workplace\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the employee presence dashboard.

    For more information, see the steps mentioned [here](set-in-office-days.md#choicetable_presence_dashboard).

2.  Navigate to the day that you want to update.

    -   Use the navigation arrows to change the day or week of the calendar.

        ![Navigation arrows on the Employee Presence dashboard.](../images/workplaceConcei-EmpPresArrow.png)

    -   Navigate to the current day by selecting the date link.
3.  From the drop-down list of the calendar card, select one of the following options.

    -   Select **Remote** to change your presence to remote.
    -   Select **In-office** to change your presence to your assigned office.
    -   Select **Visit another office** to change your presence to an office different from your assigned office, and then select the office that you’ll visit.
    An exception is automatically created for the selected day and your presence is updated.

4.  Select **Make a reservation** to reserve a space for the day you're visiting the office.

    For information about making a reservation, see [Create a reservation](../../wsd-reservation-management/task/create-reservation-request-1.md).
