# Additional requirement form

The Additional requirement form specifies visitor information that is required while adding a visitor.

<table id="table_kqq_qdr_p3c"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Name

</td><td>

Name of the additional requirement.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Visit requirements

</td><td>

Visit requirement record linked to the additional requirement record.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Order

</td><td>

Order of the additional requirement on the portal pages.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Filled by

</td><td>

Person who fills the required information.You can select the host, guest, or both.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Help text

</td><td>

Informational text for the information field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Label

</td><td>

Label text that is displayed on the portal pages.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Requirement type

</td><td>

Type of the requirement, which determines the section that the information field is displayed in.You can select any one of the following options:

-   Additional information: Information like diet restrictions or license plate number; displayed in the first section of the pre-check tasks.
-   Document to upload: Documents like a visitor ID or photograph; displayed in the second section of the pre-check tasks.
-   Document to acknowledge: Policy documents or guidelines; displayed in the third section of the pre-check tasks.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Active

</td><td>

Option to set the record to active. Inactive records are not displayed on the portal pages.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Mandatory for

</td><td>

Person who is required to fill the information.You can select the host, guest, both, or none.

If you select **None**, the field is considered optional.

</td></tr></tbody>
