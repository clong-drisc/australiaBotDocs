# Visitor registration states

The visitor logs display the current state of visitor registrations. These states can be changed to other states.

The different states of a visitor registration are explained as follows.

<table id="table_j4m_sqt_dnb"><thead><tr><th>

State

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Planned

</td><td>

This is the initial state of a visitor registration after it is created.This state can be changed to the following states:

-   Check-in
-   Cancel
-   Didn't show up

</td></tr><tr><td>

Checked in

</td><td>

The visitor is checked in by the reception staff.This state can be changed to the following states

-   Check out
-   Break check out

You can print a visitor badge by selecting **Print badge**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Checked out

</td><td>

The visitor is checked out by the reception staff.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Break checked in

</td><td>

The visitor is checked in by the reception staff after the visitor returned from a break.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Break checked out

</td><td>

The visitor is checked out by the reception staff after the visitor went on a break.This state can be updated to the following states

-   Break check in
-   Check out

</td></tr><tr><td>

Completed

</td><td>

The visitor has completed the visit and checked out.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Cancelled

</td><td>

The visitor registration is cancelled.

</td></tr><tr><td>

No show

</td><td>

The visitor didn't show up.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Disappeared

</td><td>

There is no update about the visitor or has checked out without updating the reception staff.

</td></tr></tbody>
