# Location Policy form

Use the location policy form to assign a visitor policy to a workplace location, apply the policy to internal/external visitors and configure whether the policies require signatures.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Location|Workplace location to which the visitor policy it linked.|
|Policy|Visitor policy that is linked being linked to the workplace location.|
|Active|Option to activate the record.|
|Required for internal|Option to enable the policy for internal visitors.|
|Require signature for internal|Option to enable signatures for internal visitors to acknowledge the policy. The visitor signature is stored in the visitor policy confirmation record.|
|Required for external|Option to enable the policy for external visitors.|
|Require signature for external|Option to enable signatures for external visitors to acknowledge the policy. The visitor signature is stored in the visitor policy confirmation record.|
