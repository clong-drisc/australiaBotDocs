# Use the receptionist portal

Use the receptionist portal to manage visitor activity in your workplace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_visitor.staff

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  On the Employee Center portal, select **Help Center** &gt; **Workplace Services**.

3.  From the Workplace Services, select **Manage receptionist desk**.

    The receptionist portal page opens with a dashboard of visits for the current day.

4.  Change the location of the portal from the location list under the `Visitors` header label.

    The locations in the list are fetched from the workplace profile of the receptionist. The primary workplace location is selected by default.

5.  Perform any of the following actions based on your preference.

**Complete pre-check tasks**

</td><td>

2.  On the details panel, select **Open tasks**.

The visitor might have to provide a signature based on the configuration set by the admin.

**Check-in a visitor**

</td><td>

2.  Verify that the visitor has completed their pre check-in tasks.

If the pre-check is not complete, the visitor can complete their tasks on the portal.

3.  On the visitor card or the details panel, select **Check in**.

Alternatively, you can drag the visitor card into the checked in visitors column.

 You can undo a check-in by selecting **Undo Check-In** from the context menu in the details panel.
