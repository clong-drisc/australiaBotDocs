# View entry requests

Filter the list of entry requests that were completed to evaluate different areas of your return to work progress. Entry requests are the records submitted by the health screener for each employee who is screened before entry.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_imt\_monitoring.monitoring\_admin

## About this task

For example, you could see records for employees in San Diego who were not granted access, and compare the reasons for those denials. The reason for denial is either no personal protective equipment \(PPE\) or high temperature.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Health Screening** &gt; **Entry Requests**.

    ![List of requests for entries.](../image/entry-request-list.png)

2.  To evaluate request for entry data, build a query or sort and group the list by the appropriate columns.
