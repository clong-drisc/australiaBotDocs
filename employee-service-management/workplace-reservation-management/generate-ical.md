# Download an iCalendar for a reservation

Add the reservation to your office calendar by downloading the iCalendar of the reservation.

## Before you begin

You can download an iCalendar only if your organization has enabled the configuration on the reserved workplace item.

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.workplace\_user

## About this task

Download an iCalendar of a reservation directly from the Reservation summary page. After you download the iCalendar, an `.ics` file is generated which opens a meeting appointment window directly in your calendar provider application. You can review the details and save to add the appointment to your calendar.

For example, assume that your calendar provider is Microsoft Outlook. After you download an iCalendar of a reservation, the iCalendar opens a new meeting appointment window directly in Microsoft Outlook. You can view the details and click **Save and close** to add the reservation to your calendar.
