# Applications

Employees can discover, access, and manage all their business applications from a single location.

The featured apps serves as a central hub where employees can discover, access, and manage all their work applications from a single location. This streamlined approach eliminates the need to remember multiple URLs or navigate through various portals to access different business applications.

![featured applications](../images/es-featured-applications.png "Featured applications")

## Key capabilities

The app provides the following capabilities:

-   Centralized application directory displaying all available business applications
-   Search functionality to quickly locate specific applications
-   Recently opened applications for faster access to frequently used tools
-   Alphabetical sorting options to organize applications systematically
-   Single sign-on \(SSO\) integration for seamless authentication
-   Application descriptions and metadata to help employees understand each tool's purpose

## Benefits

The app launcher enhances employee productivity by:

-   Reducing time spent searching for applications across different systems
-   Providing a consistent user experience across all business applications
-   Eliminating the need to bookmark or memorize multiple application URLs
-   Supporting quick discovery of new applications as they become available
-   Maintaining application usage history for improved workflow efficiency

## Supported application types

The app launcher supports various types of business applications that you use in your organization.

-   Human resources and payroll systems \(ADP, Workday\)
-   Communication and collaboration tools \(MS Teams, Zoom, Outlook\)
-   Financial and expense management platforms \(Concur, Fidelity\)
-   Learning and development resources \(LinkedIn Learning\)
-   File storage and sharing services \(OneDrive\)
-   Recognition and engagement platforms \(Culture Amp, You earned it\)
-   Procurement and business management tools \(Coupa, Smartrecruiters\)
-   Volunteer and community engagement applications \(Benevity\)

**Note:** Application availability depends on your organization subscriptions and SSO configuration. Contact your system administrator if you cannot access a required application.
