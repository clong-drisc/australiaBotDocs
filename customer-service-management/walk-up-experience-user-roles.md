# Walk-up Experience for CSM user roles

Walk-up Experience roles for different users who interact with in-person support queues.

|Role Name|Role|Description|
|---------|----|-----------|
|sn\_walkup.walkup\_login|Store representative|Helps requester check in at the walk-up location.|
|sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_technician|CSM walk-up technician|Fulfills user demands at the CSM walk-up location.|
|sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_manager|CSM walk-up manager|Manages the team at the walk-up location.|
|sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_admin|CSM walk-up administrator|Administers walk-up interactions, location queues, and walk-up reasons.|
