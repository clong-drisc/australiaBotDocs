# Guest users experience on Consumer Portal

Guest users can view the header, footer, knowledge articles cards, and resources menu on the home page.

Guest users landing on the Consumer Portal can access the home page and several public elements, including the header, menu, footer, and banner widgets, which feature the **Browse Services** and **FAQs** buttons.

They can also view taxonomy topic cards showcasing public content, such as articles and catalog items, and the Knowledge quick link widget displaying public articles.

However, on selecting any card, button, or menu items, the guest users are redirected to the login page.
