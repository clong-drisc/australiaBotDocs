# Create a topic

Create a topic for users to create and share content.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Topics**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    For field description, see [Topic form fields](../reference/topic-form-fields.md).

4.  Click **Submit**.

    The topic is created and added to the list of topics.


## What to do next

Add a topic to a forum. For details, see [Add a topic to a forum](add-topic-to-forum.md).
