# Copy permissions from a parent forum

You can copy permissions from a parent forum to a child forum.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.forum\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Forums**.

2.  Click a forum.

3.  Click the **Copy Permissions from Parent Forum** related link.

    All permissions and content types are copied from the parent to the child forum. A summary of the results is displayed at the top of the form.


## What to do next

Configure the forum users and permissions as required.
