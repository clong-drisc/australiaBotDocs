# Edit an event

Edit the details of past and upcoming events. By editing a past event, you can attach a document or share the meeting recording link with your event subscribers.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.community\_user, sn\_communities.moderation\_admin, sn\_communities.community\_moderator, sn\_communities.forum\_moderator, or sn\_communities.forum\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Search for an event that you want to modify.

2.  Click the More Options icon \(![More Options.](../images/moreoptions-icon.png)\) and then click **Edit**.

3.  On the form, edit the fields as required.
