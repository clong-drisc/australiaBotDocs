# View community logs

Community logs display information about who has viewed or searched for content in a community. The list view displays all log entries, unless filtered.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Search Logs** or **View Logs**.

2.  View the logs.

    |Log|Description|
    |---|-----------|
    |Search Log|View who has searched for what and where in the community.|
    |View Log|View who has viewed what and where in the community.|
