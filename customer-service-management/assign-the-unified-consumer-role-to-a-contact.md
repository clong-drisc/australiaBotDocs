# Assign the consumer role to a contact

Assign the Consumer \(sn\_customerservice.consumer\) role to the customer\_contact to designate them as a consumer in the Customer Service Management \(CSM\) application.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Customer Service** &gt; **Customer** &gt; **Contacts**.

2.  Select a contact from the contact records.

3.  In the Roles related list, select **Edit**.

4.  In the Collection list, select the Consumer \(sn\_customerservice.consumer\) role and move it to the Roles list.

5.  Select **Save**.


## Result

Selected contact is now both a consumer and a contact.
