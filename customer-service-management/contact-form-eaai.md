# Contact form for Email Interaction for CSM

The Contact form enables you to add the customer contact to the interaction record.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|First name|First name of the customer contact.|
|Last name|Last name of the customer contact.|
|Account|Account with which the customer contact is associated.|
|Title|Title of the customer contact.|
|Language|Preferred language of the customer for all communications and interactions.|
|Email|Email address of the customer contact.|
|Business phone|Business phone number of the customer contact.|
|Mobile phone|Mobile phone number of the customer contact.|
|Time zone|Time zone of the customer contact.|
|Notification|Enable notifications for the customer contact using the drop-down list.|
