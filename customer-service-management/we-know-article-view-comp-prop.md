# Component properties for the Knowledge article view

Component property enables you to configure the behavior of the Knowledge article view component instance on the third-party website.

|Component property|Description|
|------------------|-----------|
|Record ID|Sys ID of the record.|
|Hide header|Option to show or hide the article header and its components.|
|Hide language selector|Option to show or hide the language selector when multiple languages are available for the article.|
|Hide author information|Option to show or hide author information from article.|
|Hide reading time|Option to show or hide the reading time on the article header.|
|Hide last updated|Option to show or hide the last updated time on the article header.|
|Hide view count|Option to show or hide the view count on the article header.|
|Hide average rating|Option to show or hide the average rating on the article header.|
|Hide attachments|Option to show or hide article attachments.|
|Hide helpful option|Option to show or hide the feedback option on the article page.|
|Hide article rating|Option to show or hide the option to rate the article.|
|Disable displaying latest article version|Option to show or hide the article version based on the article sys ID.|
|Hide errors|Option to show or hide the error messages from end user when the component fails to load initially.|
|Hide notifications|Option to show or hide the notifications from end user.|
