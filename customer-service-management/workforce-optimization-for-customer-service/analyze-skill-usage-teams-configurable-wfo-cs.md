# Analyze skills for your teams in Workforce Optimization for Customer Service

Add skills to groups and view skill assignments for your teams.

## Before you begin

Role required: skill\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Manager Workspace**.

2.  Click the Teams icon \(![Teams icon.](../image/teams-new.png)\).

3.  Select a team for which you want to analyze skills.

4.  Add a skill to the team.

    1.  In the **Skills** tab, click **New**.
    2.  Select a skill in the **Skill** field.
    3.  In the **Skill level** field, select a level for the selected skill.
    4.  In the **Inherited from** field, select the skill from which this skill is inherited.
    5.  Click **Save**.
    You can click on a skill to analyze skill data such as the number of users or tasks with that skill, the overall skill coverage, and how many experts you currently have with that skill.
