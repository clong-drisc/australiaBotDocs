# Create a social media profile

Users with the customer service manager role can create a social media profile for an account, contact, or consumer.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice\_manager or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to an Account, Contact, or Consumer record.

    -   **Customer Service** &gt; **Customer** &gt; **Accounts**
    -   **Customer Service** &gt; **Customer** &gt; **Contacts**
    -   **Customer Service** &gt; **Customer** &gt; **Consumers**
2.  In the **Social Profiles** related list on the selected record, click **New**.

3.  Fill in the fields on the Social Profile form, as appropriate.
