# Log in to the Consumer Portal

Log in to the Consumer Portal using the name and password you created during registration.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.consumer

## Procedure

1.  Go to the Consumer Portal.

    You can access the Consumer Portal using the URL, &lt;servicenow instance URL&gt;/consumer\_portal.

2.  Select **Log in** in the portal header.

3.  Enter username and password on the Log in pop-up.

4.  Select **Log in**.


## Result

You’re logged in to the Consumer Portal and your name and profile picture appears in the portal header.
