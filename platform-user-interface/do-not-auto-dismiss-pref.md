# Do not auto-dismiss page alerts preference

Learn more about the Do not auto-dismiss page alerts accessibility preference.

![Do not auto-dismiss page alerts accessibility preference.](../image/coral-pref-auto-dismiss.png)

The purpose of this preference is to verify users are aware of critical information and can take necessary actions.

<table id="table_ayc_pgy_hgc"><thead><tr><th>

Detail

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Feature

</td><td>

Keeps page alerts visible until acknowledged.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Who benefits

</td><td>

Users who require explicit notification of important information.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Visual representation

</td><td>

**Activated:** Page alerts remain visible until manually dismissed.

 **Deactivated:** Page alerts may disappear after a certain time.
