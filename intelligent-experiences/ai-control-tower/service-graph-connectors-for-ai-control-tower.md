# Service Graph Connectors for AI Control Tower

You can use Service Graph Connectors \(SGC\)  to import and integrate third-party data into  CMDB  and non-CMDB  tables to create AI connections.

## How SGC works

When you access third-party APIs, they return data. This data must be stored in ServiceNow tables. Before saving it, we should transform the data from its JSON format into a preferred format and then store it in the ServiceNow tables.

## Benefits of SGC

Lets explore some of the major benefits:

-   Gain trust in your CMDB data
-   Minimize operational risk
-   Minimize custom integrations
-   Accelerate time to value

To understand benefits and features of SGC in detail, see [Service Graph Connectors](https://www.servicenow.com/products/service-graph-connectors.html)
