# DevOps Change Velocity

With ServiceNow® DevOps Change Velocity, you can import data from your DevOps tool chain to the ServiceNow platform and deliver changes faster without sacrificing compliance.

You can automatically create change requests from your Continuous Integration \(CI\) and Continuous Deployment \(CD\) pipelines and then automatically approve the change requests based on policies. This enables developers to remain in their tools while providing visibility, accelerate the time required to approve change requests, and provide an audit trail.
