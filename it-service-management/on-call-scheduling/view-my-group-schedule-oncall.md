# View my group schedules

You can see the on-call schedules for your groups.

## Before you begin

Role required: itil

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **On-Call Scheduling** &gt; **My Group Schedules**.

2.  Open a schedule.

3.  To view a roster, click its order number in the **Rosters** related list.
