# Use the Procurement Overview module

Use the gauges on the Procurement Overview homepage to help you track and manage requests, purchase orders, and other important aspects of the procurement process.

## Before you begin

Role required: procurement\_admin or procurement\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Procurement** &gt; **Overview**.

2.  Click elements within the gauges to obtain more information.

    For example, click a request number to view the request record.
