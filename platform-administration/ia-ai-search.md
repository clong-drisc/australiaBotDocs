# AI Search

Configure and maintain the AI-Search engine to experience a modern search experience.

## External sources

Connect your external data sources with ServiceNow to enhance self-service capabilities across your organization.

**Warning:** External Content Connector apps are on different versions and can cause crawl failures. Please update all apps to same version.
