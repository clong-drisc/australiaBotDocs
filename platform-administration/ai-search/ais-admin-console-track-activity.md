# Track your AI Search activity

View key analytics for AI Search usage, or follow a link to the full AI Search dashboard.

## Before you begin

Role required: ais\_admin

## About this task

The **Track AI Search activity pane** pane displays reports summarizing your AI Search configurations and trends.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **AI Search Admin** &gt; **AI Search Admin Home**.

2.  In the **Home** page's **Track AI Search activity** pane, review the dashboard reports.

3.  To view the full AI Search dashboard, select the **Go to full dashboard** link.

    For details on this dashboard's reports, see [AI Search dashboard](../../../use/dashboards/application-content-packs/ai-search-dashboard.md).
