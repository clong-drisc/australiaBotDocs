# SPW Jira Integrations

Improve visibility from strategy to execution with Jira Integration. This integration enables bi-directional syncing of Epics, Features, Capabilities, and Stories. While sprints are created in Enterprise Agile Planning \(EAP\) and synced to Jira, sprint status updates flow back from Jira to EAP.
