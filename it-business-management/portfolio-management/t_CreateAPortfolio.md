# Create a portfolio

Create a portfolio to manage related programs, projects, and demands.

## Before you begin

-   Create demands, programs, and projects to include as part of the portfolio.
-   Role required: it\_portfolio\_manager or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Project** &gt; **Portfolios** &gt; **Create New**.

2.  Enter a unique **Name** that summarizes the programs, projects, and demands included in this portfolio.

3.  Enter a **Description** for the portfolio that adequately explains the various programs, projects, and demands attached to it.

4.  Enter the name of the **Portfolio Manager**.

5.  Select **Create Portfolio**.


## Result

The portfolio is created and the form reopens with additional fields, related links, and related lists.
