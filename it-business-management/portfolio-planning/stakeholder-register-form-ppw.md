# Stakeholder register form

The stakeholder register form information is used to add users to the stakeholder registry.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Number|Unique identification number for the stakeholder. This number is automatically generated when a new entry is created.|
|User|Name of user being added to the stakeholder registry.|
|Portfolio|Portfolio to which the user is assigned. A group of activities managed to align to business or operational objectives constitutes a portfolio. Typically, portfolios are named according to the business units of an organization.|
|Level of Interest|Level of interest that the stakeholder has in pursuing the demand.|
|Assessment recipient|Indication of whether the user is authorized to receive assessment questionnaires for a demand.|
|Approver|Indication of whether the user has authority to approve demands.|
|Influence|Level of influence the user has over the group assessing the demand.|
|Engagement|Indication of the way the user is engaged with the demand.|
|Function|Function of the user in the demand process.|
