# Strategic Plan form

Use the Strategic Plan form to define the purpose of your organization, its strategic priorities, and the associated goals.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the strategic plan.|
|Sponsor|Sponsor for the strategic plan.|
|Start date|Start date for the strategic plan.|
|End date|End date for the strategic plan.|
|Description|A detailed description of the strategic plan.|
|Vision|A brief description about where your organization’s business will be at some point in the future, based on its strategies and associated goals.|
|Mission|A brief description about what your organization’s objectives are and its approach to reach those objectives.|
