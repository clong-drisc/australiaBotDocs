# Create monetary benefit plans for your projects in Project Workspace

Create and manage monetary benefit plans to capture the potential benefits while executing your projects.

## Before you begin

-   [Enable monetary benefit plans for projects](enable-benefitplans-pws-fin.md)
-   Role required: sn\_align\_ws.spw\_financial\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Project Workspace** and open a project.

2.  Create a monetary benefit plan using one of the options.
