# Track a hardware asset shipment

Track the progress of your hardware asset shipment that isn't delivered and that has a carrier associated with an active integration profile.

## Before you begin

Role required: inventory\_user \(read\) or inventory\_admin.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Hardware Asset Workspace** &gt; **Asset operations**.

2.  From the **Shipment** list, select **Shipments**.

3.  Select a shipment record that you want to track.

4.  Select **Track now**.


## Result

The following fields on the Shipment details form show the tracking details received from the carrier:

-   Carrier status
-   Carrier pick-up date
-   Carrier delivered date
