# Record time worked on asset repair tasks in the Hardware Asset Workspace

Manage and record time worked on Troubleshoot asset, Repair asset, and Evaluate asset tasks in the Hardware Asset Workspace. After you start work on a repair asset task, you can pause and resume work. You can also record the time worked manually.

-   **[Record time worked on a repair task automatically in the Hardware Asset Workspace](../task/record-total-repair-time-ham-ws.md)**  
Record the time that you took to complete a repair task without taking any break until you completed the task.
-   **[Record time worked on a repair task manually in the Hardware Asset Workspace](../task/record-time-worked-manually-ham-ws.md)**  
Create an entry for the time worked on an asset repair task manually in the Hardware Asset Workspace.
-   **[Pause a repair task in the Hardware Asset Workspace](../task/pause-repair-task-ham-ws.md)**  
Record a break from a repair task in the Hardware Asset Workspace if you’re not continuing to work on it. Resume the task when you start working on it again. The system automatically tracks and calculates the actual time taken to complete the task even though the work was interrupted.
