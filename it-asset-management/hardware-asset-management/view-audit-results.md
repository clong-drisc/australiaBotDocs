# View audit results

View the audit results after you audit your inventory. The Audit Results section is activated and shows the details of the audit result.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

The audit results are generated by matching the assets scanned in the stockroom or physical location with the asset records available in the ServiceNow instance.

## Procedure

1.  Open an asset audit record.
