# Publish a catalog workflow

Publish the catalog workflow to make it available for use in the system.

## Before you begin

Role required: workflow\_creator, workflow\_admin, workflow\_publisher, or admin

## About this task

After adding activities to your workflow, publish it to make it available for use.

## Procedure

1.  Open the workflow actions menu on the left beside the workflow name.

2.  Click **Publish** to publish the workflow.
