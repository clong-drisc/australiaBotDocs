# Test a survey URL

When there are survey users associated with a survey, the survey is restricted so only those users can use the survey URL. You can test the survey URL to ensure it is restricted.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or survey\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Survey** &gt; **View Surveys** and open a survey for which you are not a survey user.

2.  Under **Related Links**, click **View Survey URL**.

3.  Copy the URL and click **OK** or **Cancel**.

4.  Navigate to the URL.

    You see a message that you are not authorized to take the survey.
