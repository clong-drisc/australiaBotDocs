# Enable a quiz retake

You can configure a quiz to allow recipients to resubmit their answers as many times as they like, until the quiz's due date.

## Before you begin

Role required: assessment\_admin or admin

## About this task

Results are not calculated until the quiz's configured duration has elapsed. The card in the user's queue remains visible until the quiz's due date and displays a button to allow retakes.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Assessments** &gt; **Metric Definition** &gt; **Types**.

2.  Remove the **Evaluation method = Assessment** filter condition so you can see all the records in the list.

3.  Open the quiz.

4.  In the [Assessment Metric Type](../concept/c_MetricTypesAndAssessableRecords.md) form, select the **Allow retake** check box and save the record.
