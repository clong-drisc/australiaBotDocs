# Leave a team

You can give up membership to a team by leaving that team.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  In the sidebar, click your profile picture, then select the **Teams** tab.

3.  Click **Leave** beside the team name.
