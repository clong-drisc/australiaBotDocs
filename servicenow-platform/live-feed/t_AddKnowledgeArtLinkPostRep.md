# Add a knowledge article link to a post or reply

Use the ID of a knowledge article to post it as a reply to a Live Feed conversation.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  While composing a post or reply, type the ID of the knowledge article you want to link to.

    You do not need to identify the knowledge article ID as a hyperlink. Live feed recognizes it as such and automatically converts it into a link when the message is posted.

2.  When you are finished entering your message, click **Post** or **Reply**.
