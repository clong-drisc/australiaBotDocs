# Leave a feed

Members can choose to leave a group feed.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click either **Group Feeds** or **Record Feeds**, and then select the feed name.

3.  Click **Leave**.

    **Note:** If you leave a [feed](../concept/c_RecordFeeds.md) associated with a record, such as an incident, you automatically rejoin that feed if you modify the associated record again.
