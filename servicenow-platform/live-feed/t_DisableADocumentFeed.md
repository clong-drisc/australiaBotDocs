# Disable a document feed

You can disable a document feed from the form of any table.

## Before you begin

Role required: personalize\_dictionary or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **System Definition** &gt; **Tables**.

2.  Open the table record.

3.  Clear the **Live feed** check box.

4.  Click **Update**.
