# Use knowledge on mobile devices

All users can access knowledge from mobile devices to search for and view knowledge articles.

You can also view recently viewed and most popular articles, search for articles, browse articles by category, and provide feedback to knowledge articles from Now Mobile. For information, see [Using Now Mobile for Knowledge Management](mobile-experience-for-km.md).

**Parent Topic:**[Using Knowledge Management](using-knowledge-management.md)

## Browse knowledge on mobile devices

You can browse knowledge bases using a mobile device.

Navigate to **SELF-SERVICE** &gt; **Knowledge Base**.

![Mobile Knowledge view](../image/MobileKnowledgeView.png "Mobile Knowledge View")

Browse Knowledge by tapping on a category and browsing articles or subcategories.

![Knowledge Mobile UI knowledge categories](../image/MobileKnowledgeCategoryView.png "Mobile Knowledge Categories")

**Note:** Select the desired category and click the close icon \(![Knowledge Management Mobile UI close icon](../image/KnowledgeMobileUICloseIcon.png)\) for the selected category to reflect in the search results.

## View knowledge articles on mobile devices

You can read knowledge content using a mobile device.

Tap a title to view the article.

**Note:** You cannot edit or add comments to knowledge articles on the mobile interface.

![Mobile Knowledge article view page](../image/MobileKnowledgeArticleView.png "Mobile Knowledge Article View")

## Search knowledge on mobile devices

Find a specific article by searching on a particular term.

![Mobile Knowledge search page](../image/MobileKnowledgeSearch.png "Mobile Knowledge Search")
