# View a data collection job event

Job events show which jobs have been executed for Performance Analytics and which actions have been triggered in your ServiceNow instance, such as notifications or business rules.

## Before you begin

Role required: pa\_data\_collector or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Performance Analytics** &gt; **Data Collector** &gt; **Job Events**.

2.  Click **Created** to view the details of a specific job event.

    Additional information on the job event is displayed.
