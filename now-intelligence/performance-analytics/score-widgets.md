# Score widgets

Score widgets show aggregate indicator scores.

| |Visualization|Description|
|---|-------------|-----------|
|![Latest score icon](../image/latest-score-icon.png)|[Latest score](../task/create-latest-score-widget.md)|Shows a single indicator score and the change in that score compared to a previous period. A line representing the time series of scores appears in the background.|
|![speedometer icon](../image/speedometer-icon.png)|[Speedometer](../task/create-speedometer-widget.md)|Shows the most recently collected score of an indicator in the form of a round meter. The indicator target and its color scheme are shown on the dial.|
|![Data visualization dial type - med](../../reporting/image/inline-data-vis-dial.png)|[Dial](../task/create-speedometer-widget.md)|Shows an overview of the most recently collected score of an indicator in a half circle.|
|![](../image/real-time-score-icon.png)|[Real-time score](../task/create-real-time-score-widget.md)|Shows the current indicator score and the change in that score compared to the score at the last scheduled collection. A line representing the time series of scores appears in the background.|
