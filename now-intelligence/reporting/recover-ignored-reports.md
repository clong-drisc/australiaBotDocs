# Reassess ignored reports

When you address the ACL Assessment for Reports, you can ignore any report. View these reports to consider their statuses again.

## Before you begin

Roles required: admin and security\_admin.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Reports** &gt; **ACL Assessment for Reports** &gt; **Ignored Reports and Users List**.

2.  In the Ignore Report column, set the value of the reports to reassess to `false`.

    ![](../image/rep-acl-reassess-ignored.png)


## Result

The ACL Assessment for Reports assesses the `false` reports the next time you run the assessment.
