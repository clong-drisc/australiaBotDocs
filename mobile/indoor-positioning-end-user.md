# Indoor positioning

If an admin has configured your mobile app for indoor positioning, you can get guided directions in real time to a resource on your device's Indoor Wayfinding map.

Indoor positioning includes these following features.

## View a guided path to your destination and the estimated travel time, distance, and arrival time
