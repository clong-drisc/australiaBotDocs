# Find rules that will be applied to an event

View the rules that will be applied on an event to determine how this event will be processed.

## Before you begin

Role required: evt\_mgmt\_admin, evt\_mgmt\_operator, or evt\_mgmt\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Event Management** &gt; **All Events**, and then click an event number.

2.  Under **Related Links**, click **Find matching rules**.

3.  Review the information that appears in the blue box at the top of the page, and review all **Processing Notes** to find rules that apply to an event.
