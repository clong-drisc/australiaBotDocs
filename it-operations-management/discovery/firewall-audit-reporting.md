# Firewall Audits and Reporting

Use the Firewall Audits and Reporting application to explore and create an inventory of your firewall security policies, devices, device groups, and manager information. Additionally, you can initiate requests for new firewall rules through the Service Catalog application and conduct audits of firewall security policies within specified time frames.
