# View subscription statistics for ITOM

View how many subscriptions for IT Operations Management applications your organization purchased and consumed.

## Before you begin

Ensure that your organization has purchased ITOM subscriptions. You cannot view the information in the **ITOM License** module without subscriptions.

Ensure that you installed the latest available version of the ITOM/OT SU Licensing from ServiceNow Store.

Role required: sn\_itom\_license.reader

## About this task

Review the configuration item \(CI\) allocation and allocation level to see how your organization uses IT Operations Management subscriptions and plan upcoming subscription needs.

## Procedure

1.  To view subscription information for IT Operations Management subscriptions purchased a la carte and in bundles, navigate to **ITOM License** &gt; **License Summary**.

    Use subscription names to differentiate between subscriptions bought in bundles and a la carte.

2.  Review the information on the form as covered in [Subscriptions form for the ITOM products](../reference/itom-subscriptions-form.md).
