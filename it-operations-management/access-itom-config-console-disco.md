# Access the ITOM Configuration Console

Access the IT Operations Management Configuration console to set up Discovery.

## Before you begin

Verify that the ITOM Visibility and Now Assist for IT Operations Management plugins are installed. For more information, see [Install ITOM Visibility using Now Assist for Setup](install-nowassist-setup-itom-visibility.md) and [Install Now Assist for IT Operations Management](install-now-assist-itom.md).

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Admin** &gt; **Admin Home** on your instance.

2.  On the IT Operations Management tile, select **View product overview**.

    The IT Operations Management page in the Product Hub displays.

    ![IT Operations Management page with installed plugins and Configure button to access the Configure IT Operations Management page.](../image/configure-itom.png)

3.  Select **Configure**.

    The Configure IT Operations Management page displays.

    ![The Configure IT Operations Management page where you can access the Discovery and Event Management modules.](../image/aiops-admin-conf-itom-page.png)
