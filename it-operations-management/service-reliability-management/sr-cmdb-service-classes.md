# CMDB service classes

Service Reliability Management \(SRM\) typically supports several Configuration Management Database \(CMDB\) service classes.

Types of CMDB service classes that SRM supports:

-   Service instances \(cmdb\_ci\_service\_auto\)
-   Technology management services \(cmdb\_ci\_service\_technical\)
-   Query-based services \(cmdb\_ci\_query\_based\_service\)
-   Mapped application services \(cmdb\_ci\_service\_discovered\)
-   Calculated application services \(cmdb\_ci\_calculated\)
-   Tag-based application services \(cmdb\_ci\_service\_by\_tags\)
